# 🏥 QueueCure AI – Smart Clinic Queue & Patient Flow Management

QueueCure AI is an AI-powered digital queue management platform that transforms traditional hospital waiting systems into an intelligent, real-time patient flow experience. It reduces waiting uncertainty, improves doctor utilization, and enhances patient communication through AI-based routing, multilingual voice announcements, and live queue synchronization.

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Most hospitals and clinics still rely on manual token systems, causing long waiting times, poor queue visibility, inefficient doctor allocation, and frustrated patients.

QueueCure AI addresses these challenges by providing an intelligent queue management system that automatically assigns patients to doctors, predicts waiting times, prioritizes emergencies, and keeps both patients and hospital staff synchronized in real time.

2. Register a new patient from the Reception Dashboard.
3. Verify AI doctor assignment.
4. Call the next patient.
5. Open the Waiting Room page.
6. Observe real-time token updates.
7. Test multilingual voice announcements.
8. Mark patient arrival.
9. Complete consultation.
10. Test doctor availability and patient reassignment.

# ⚠️ Known Limitations

* Voice announcements depend on browser speech synthesis support.
* Queue data is stored in memory (database integration planned).
* Estimated waiting times improve with larger consultation history.
* Internet connection is required for real-time synchronization.
